At Yokohama English Church, we are a warm and welcoming international community that embraces people from all walks of life -especially those who have left their home countries to build a life here in Japan. We exist to foster spiritual growth, community connection, and the love of God in the hearts of everyone we meet.

As a ministry of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, our Pathfinder Club provides a vibrant, Christ-centered program for children and teens. Through fun activities, outdoor adventures, service projects, and Bible-based learning, our goal is to guide young people toward developing strong character, a spirit of service, and most importantly, a personal relationship with Jesus Christ.
